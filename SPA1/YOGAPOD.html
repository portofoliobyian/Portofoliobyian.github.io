Yoga Pod
This Yoga pod project is located on the site of Masagi Coffee, Ciumbuleuit, Bandung. In this design, learning is focused on the abstraction of human movement into a unique form. into a Yoga Pod that is unique in form. The lines are taken from the movements of Bikram Yoga and Ashtanga Yoga, by compositing the lines taken from these 2 poses, I create a pod. from these 2 poses, I created a pod that has a layered shape using ETF-E Membrane material to create a translucent opening that provides privacy while creating a play of shadows. that provides privacy while creating a play of shadows from the tree leaves above. Each pod will be used for one person only and placed on the site in a spread manner without facing each other so that pod users cannot be seen by other pod users.
