Revitalisasi Gor Padjajaran
This Project took place in Gor Padjajaran Bandung. The design was inspired by the Yoyogi National Gymnasium in Japan. This widespan building design involves the usage of cable structure and huge concrete structure. The shape of the building was abstracted from a traditional musical instrument of the Sunda culture, Kecapi. The strings of the kecapi is abstracted into the 20 cable structure holding the roof of the building, and the huge concrete column took form of the kecapi itself. The facilities included are Basketball court, Volleyball court, Badminton court and running track. The site is designed to keep the culinary culture alive, and making open spaces for bazaar and other communal activities.
