4 Star Hotel at Jalan Tamblong
This Project took place in Jalan Tamblong, Bandung. This 4 star Hotel design elements is very much inspired by the Pompidou Centre design which creates a sense of honesty just by viewing the exposed elements of the design, and most importantly, giving new experiences for Hotel guests and every staff working inside the hotel. The site has a class c heritage buildings that must be conserved especially the facade element. So instead of demolishing it, I decided to make it as a cafe for a bonus facility for the hotel. The shape of the building is formed by the smallest unit designed, arranged in a certain pattern creating a unique look for the facade when casts by shadows and light. The exposed plumbing is part of the design to experiment in hope of easier maintenance and new unique visual experience for everyone. putting the interior elements as a part of the facade and the other way around was the whole idea of the design. The corridor path took form from a branch of a tree, creating a new experience when you walk with the voids around you.
